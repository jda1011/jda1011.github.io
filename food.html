Taste the Flavors of Taniti

Resturants

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine. Our vistors favorite resturants are below:

  • Island Catch Bistro

    • Nestled on the along the shores of Taniti, Island Catch Bistro offers a delightful dining experience centered around the island's freshest local fish and rice dishes. With its charming, rustic decor and breathtaking ocean views, the bistro provides a serene and inviting atmosphere perfect for both casual lunches and romantic dinners.

  • Harbor Grill & Diner

    • Located in the heart of the island's tourist district, Harbor Grill & Diner is the go-to spot for classic American-style meals with a seaside twist. This cozy and welcoming eatery combines the charm of a traditional American diner with the laid-back vibes of island life, offering a perfect retreat for hungry travelers.

  • Pacific Fusion

    • Perched on a scenic cliffside with stunning views of the turquoise waters below, Pacific Fusion offers a vibrant fusion of flavors from across Asia. This modern restaurant blends traditional culinary techniques with contemporary flair, creating a unique dining experience that captivates both the palate and the senses.
